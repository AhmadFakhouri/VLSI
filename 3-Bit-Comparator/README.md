VLSI Design and Chip Simulation
This is a collection of projects where I designed and tested digital circuits at the transistor level.

Tools Used
•	Design and Testing: Tanner EDA (S-Edit, T-Spice, and W-Edit).
•	Technology: TSMC 0.35μm CMOS Library.

Main Projects
•	3-Bit Comparator: A complex system that compares two 3-bit numbers to see which is larger.
•	Logic Gate Optimization: Improved the performance of NOR and NAND gates to ensure they switch on and off at exactly 2.5V.
•	Speed Analysis: Verified that my circuits work at high speeds, with switching times as fast as 318.9 picoseconds.
